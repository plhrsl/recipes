Scrambled Eggs

Description

"When our son, Chris, wants something other than cold cereal in the morning, he whips up these eggs. Cheese and evaporated milk make them especially good. They're easy to make when you're camping, too." â€”Chris Pfleghaar, Elk River, Minnesota.

Ingredients

  • large eggs
  • 1/4 cup evaporated milk or half-and-half cream
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1/8 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon canola oil
  • 2 tablespoons cheese dip

Steps

  1. In a bowl, whisk eggs, milk, salt and pepper. In a large skillet, heat oil over medium heat. Pour in egg mixture; stir in cheese dip. Cook and stir until eggs are thickened and no liquid egg remains.
